    What is a Hackathon

    Hackathon is a sprint-like event for a group of programmers to work together on a collaborative project. Most hackathons are competitions where several teams are competing to create prototypes that innovate on a theme or improve upon an existing project.


    More about JHack

    Since this event will be organized online, you are welcomed to work and submit your project anytime within the time frame. Guest speakers and workshops will be accessible through this website. You can also seek help and assistancce through our discord channel. We are currently working on a "user portal" on this site where you will be able to have quick access to the event schedule and submit your project. Tune in for updates!

    Prompts & Potential Topics

    • Build a game that raises awareness of one of the UN Sustainable development goals
    • Build an interactive tool that presents data related to sustainability/environment
    • Create a programme to visualise some things you have learnt in your classes, such as physics motions or TOK map projections
    • Create a web scraping programme that can save time in your workflow (e.g. if you go on your school website to find the school lunch menu everyday, then try writing a programme that can fetch the menu for you)
    • Create a Discord bot for you and your friends to optimise your study sessions
